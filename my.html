Toji Fushiguro

Toji Fushiguro (伏ふし黒ぐろ甚とう爾じ Fushiguro Tōji?), born Toji Zenin (禪ぜん院いん甚とう爾じ Zen'in Tōji?) is a recurring character in the Jujutsu Kaisen series.

Character Info

Kanji: 伏黒甚爾

Alias: Sorcerer Killer

Birthday: December 31

Gender: Male

Hair Color: Black

Eye Color: Green (Manga) / Dark Blue (Anime)

Occupation: Non-Curse User

Affiliation: Formerly Zenin Clan, Independent Assassin

First Appearance: Chapter 66 / Episode 25
